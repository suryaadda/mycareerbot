## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

**Before Your First Run:**
- Upload a clean, well-formatted resume PDF
- Make sure your LinkedIn is up to date (the agent will reference it)
- Set realistic minimum salary (check levels.fyi for your role)

**During Your Job Search:**
- Run batches at the same time daily (consistency matters)
- Actually review before submitting (AI is 95% accurate, you're the 5%)
- Track everything (update statuses when companies respond)
- Follow up after 1 week of no response

**For Best Interview Rate:**
- Target 15-20 applications/day (not more, not less)
- Focus on companies that match your background
- Customize the AI-generated content slightly based on your research
- Apply within 24-48 hours of job posting (early applications win)
